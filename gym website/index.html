You can log your exercises by selecting your exercise type, setting the number of reps and sets, and entering the calories burned during your workout.

Can I track my progress over time?

Yes, you can track your progress by saving your exercise logs and reviewing them periodically to see improvements in reps, sets, and calories burned.

Is there a way to personalize my workout plan?
